Our Story

Who We Are

Sham Shawarma & Grill is a restaurant that specialises in authentic Middle Eastern cuisine, focusing especially on freshly prepared shawarma and grilled dishes. The restaurant is known for its warm atmosphere, flavorful spices, and traditional cooking techniques that reflect the rich culinary heritage of the Middle East.

At Sham Shawarma & Grill, meats such as chicken and beef are carefully marinated with aromatic herbs and spices, then slow-roasted or flame-grilled to bring out deep, savory flavours. The menu includes shawarma wraps, platters, grilled kebabs, fresh salads, hummus, garlic sauce, and warm pita bread.

The restaurant emphasises freshness and quality, preparing ingredients daily to ensure customers receive meals that are both delicious and satisfying.

Our Mission

The mission of Sham Shawarma & Grill is to serve fresh, flavourful, and authentic meals made with quality ingredients and traditional recipes.

The restaurant is committed to maintaining excellent food standards, delivering fast and friendly service, and ensuring that every customer leaves satisfied.

We believe that great food should be accessible to everyone — from casual diners to large families and corporate events.

Our Vision

The vision of Sham Shawarma & Grill is to become a trusted and well-recognised destination for authentic Middle Eastern cuisine in Durban and beyond.

The restaurant strives to create memorable dining experiences where customers can enjoy high-quality food, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere inspired by Middle Eastern hospitality.

Our Values

⭐ Quality First

Every ingredient we use is sourced fresh daily. Our chefs prepare everything in-house — from our signature garlic sauce to our slow-roasted shawarma meat. We never compromise on quality because we believe you deserve only the best.

❤ Community Roots

Sham Shawarma & Grill is proud to be part of the Durban community. We actively participate in local food drives, sponsor community events, and open our doors to volunteers who share our passion for bringing people together through food.

✅ Halal & Integrity

All our meats are 100% halal certified. We operate with transparency and integrity in every aspect of our business — from sourcing to serving. Our customers can dine with full confidence in the quality and halal status of every dish.

💡 Always Improving

We continuously refine our recipes, expand our menu, and adopt new ways to enhance your dining experience — including online ordering, catering packages, and seasonal specials. Tradition meets innovation at Sham.
