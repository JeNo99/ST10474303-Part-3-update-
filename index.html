About Us

Sham Shawarma & Grill is a family-owned restaurant serving authentic Middle Eastern flavours with a focus on expertly grilled meats and savoury shawarma. Known for its fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, Sham Shawarma & Grill offers a delicious and vibrant dining experience, bringing the tastes of the Middle East to your table.
